Delete Your Account

Agentry Inc · support@agentryinc.com

You have the right to delete your VitalNoteAI account and all associated data at any time. This page explains what gets deleted, what happens after deletion, and how to submit your request.

What gets deleted

  • Your account credentials
  • All SOAP notes you have generated
  • Your profile information

What happens after deletion

  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Voice recordings are never stored, so there is nothing to delete there
  • Processing takes up to 7 days

How to request deletion

Send an email to support@agentryinc.com with the following:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
Email Us to Delete My Account →
⚠ This action is irreversible Once your account is deleted, all your data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
