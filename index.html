The #1 differentiator

The AI that checks itself before you do.

Every other clinical note tool gives you a note and hopes you catch the errors. VitalNoteAI runs a 5-agent validation framework that detects hallucinations — information that wasn't in the transcript but appeared in the note — and flags it before you read a single word.

You get a quality score, a revision count, and specific validation concerns called out in plain language. Not a black box. A transparent tool you can actually trust.
