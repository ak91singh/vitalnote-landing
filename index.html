Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 21, 2026

Agentry Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") operates the VitalNoteAI mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use VitalNoteAI.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account we collect your email address and, optionally, a display name. This information is stored in Firebase Authentication.

Audio Recordings

When you record a consultation, audio is captured on your device and transmitted securely over HTTPS to our transcription provider (Groq) solely to generate a text transcript. Audio is not stored on our servers after transcription is complete. If you enable "Auto-Delete Audio" in settings, the local audio file is also deleted immediately after analysis.

SOAP Note Text

Generated SOAP notes are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to Agentry servers unless you explicitly share them (e.g., PDF export via the system share sheet).

Usage Metrics

We store anonymised session counts and recording durations in your device's secure storage to power the Dashboard stats (sessions, time saved). This data never leaves your device.

Subscription Status

Your plan tier and subscription expiry are stored in Firebase Firestore, linked to your user ID, to enforce usage limits and grant Pro access.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To transcribe audio and generate SOAP notes on your behalf.
  • To enforce free-tier daily limits and grant paid-plan access.
  • To respond to support requests sent to agentryinc@gmail.com.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication and Firestore database. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Groq — Audio transcription via Whisper. Audio is sent over HTTPS and is not retained by Groq beyond the API call.
  • OpenRouter — AI text generation for SOAP notes. Only anonymised transcript text is sent; no patient identifiers.

4. Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing agentryinc@gmail.com. Audio recordings are never stored beyond the duration of a single API call.

5. Security

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS. Firebase data is protected by Firestore Security Rules. We follow HIPAA-aligned minimum-data-retention principles — we store the least amount of information necessary to deliver the service.

6. Children's Privacy

VitalNoteAI is intended for licensed healthcare professionals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" at the top of this page will reflect the latest revision. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact us at agentryinc@gmail.com or write to Agentry Inc., India.

AI Processing & Third-Party Services

VitalNoteAI uses third-party artificial intelligence services to generate clinical SOAP notes from voice recordings. Specifically:

  • Voice recordings submitted for transcription are processed by Groq Inc. (api.groq.com) using their Whisper speech-to-text model.
  • Transcribed text is processed by AI language models via OpenRouter Inc. (openrouter.ai) or Groq Inc. for SOAP note generation.

Voice recordings and transcripts are transmitted to these services solely for the purpose of generating the requested SOAP note. Agentry Inc. (the developer of VitalNoteAI) does not store voice recordings on its own servers after transcription is complete.

You are the Data Fiduciary for any patient data processed through this app. By using VitalNoteAI, you confirm that you have obtained appropriate patient consent for AI-assisted documentation in accordance with applicable law, including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP), 2023 where applicable.

AI-generated notes are provided as a documentation aid only and are not a substitute for clinical judgment. The doctor is solely responsible for reviewing, verifying, and signing any generated note before use.
